About Sneaky

Meet Sneaky, the vibrant and adventurous snake from Matt Furie’s Night Riders. Debuting in this iconic book, Sneaky embodies the whimsical and surreal style that degens adore.

In Night Riders, Sneaky explores the magical nocturnal world, making friends and discovering hidden wonders under the moonlit sky. He meets Flappy, a bat-like dude who loves video games, and Draggy, a friendly wingless dragon. Along with Wat, the rat, they embark on mystical adventures together.

With Furie’s unique artistic touch, Sneaky adds a touch of wonder and joy to every page.

how to buy Sneaky

make wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

get some ETH

have ETH in your wallet to switch to $Sneaky. If you dont have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

go to uniswap

connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet.

buy Sneaky

Paste the $Sneaky token address into Uniswap, select $Sneaky COIN, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign. and then switch $ETH for $Sneaky
